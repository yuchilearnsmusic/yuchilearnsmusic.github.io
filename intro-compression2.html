What is Compression?

Volume Fader

It's just a really fast volume knob/fader that can only turn the volume down

What can it be used for?

What can it be used for?

Volume Problems

It can be used to solve volume-related issues! For example...

Shaping Envelopes

Shaping the attack and sustain of a sound

Evening Out Volume

Lowering the volume of loud peaks so that the track's volume is more consistent

Creating Depth

Manipulating the volume of tracks to make them sound closer or more far away from the listener

Parallel Compression

Making a track sound closer to the listener whilst keeping the dynamics of the original signal

De-Essing

Lowering the volume of harsh high-frequency sounds (such as when singers sing consonant sounds like 's')
